Who We Are?

PAUL OSAJI & CO is a leading real estate consultancy firm, providing exceptional services since 1997. With our headquarters in Lekki Phase 1, Lagos, and additional offices in Ikeja, Asaba, and Abuja, we offer comprehensive solutions nationwide. Registered with the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Nigeria (CAP E 13 LFN 2007).

OUR MISSION

“To provide premium and innovative real estate consultancy services with the aim of giving our very best at all times to the benefit of all stakeholders and to be the industry First rate in Nigeria and beyond.”

OUR VISION

“To build a team that consistently challenges itself to deliver limitless possibilities to our clients and to give them maximum value for their investment.”

CORE VALUES

> Seek to establish a culture that will foster dynamism, high ethical standards, professionalism, and teamwork.
> Making performance a daily commitment.
> Achieve differentiation from competitors by developing an uncompromising commitment to superior quality in all aspects of our services.
> Seek to understand and show responsiveness to the needs of our clients and ensure their satisfaction.

OUR TEAM OF PROFESSIONALS

At Paul Osaji & Co, we take professionalism very seriously. That is why our recruitment policy is vigorously geared towards attracting highly qualified personnel. We also create a conducive environment for them to learn and ensure they are constantly exposed to global training in existing and emerging areas of competence.

Our Partners and Surveyors have considerable experience spanning every sector of the real estate profession. We have in the course of our practice performed appraisal/valuation and other real estate assignments for a host of high-net-worth individuals and corporate organizations in various parts of the country.
