About Paul Osaji & Co

Established in 1997, Paul Osaji & Co is a premier firm of Estate Surveyors and Valuers headquartered in Lekki Phase 1, Lagos, with branches in Ikeja, Asaba, and Abuja. Registered with the Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Nigeria (CAP E13 LFN 2007), we provide exceptional real estate consultancy services tailored to create value for stakeholders. Our integrated solutions, backed by market insights, ensure clients receive top-tier professional services.
